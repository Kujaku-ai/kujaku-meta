Kinpaku · reserved use only

Gold is not a palette color — it is an occasion. Reserved for things that must feel rare: flagship publications, annual covers, the single number on a page that matters. Never for body text, UI chrome, or decorative accents. Never paired with chili red in the same element.

Type 活字 · katsuji

Sentient meets mincho.

English in Sentient (display, headers). Japanese in Shippori Mincho (kanji, hiragana, katakana). Body in Satoshi. Numerals in JetBrains Mono so the columns line up.

Mincho is the Japanese equivalent of a serif — sharp triangle terminals on horizontals, contrast strokes. Sits next to Sentient like a sibling, not a costume.

Nav 線 · sen

Rail and masthead.

Two-piece brutalist navigation: left rail expands on hover, top masthead hides on scroll-down. Terminal/mono aesthetic. Rail is 64px collapsed and 180px expanded. Brand ships the CSS; the scroll-hide requires ~15 lines of JS in the consuming app. See STYLE.md /components Nav for full specification.
